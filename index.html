Q&A

Q: Will the beat have any voice tags (e.g. "jozu going crazy") after purchase?

A: No. All beats will be delivered completely untagged with every lease. A single tag is included with the trackouts though, if you'd like to add it (I'd appreciate it).

Q: What's the difference between MP3 and WAV?

A: The MP3 files you'll get from us are high quality (320kbps), but still MP3 files are inherently compressed and therefore will have a slight quality loss. WAV files on the other hand are inherently uncompressed/lossless, meaning there's no loss in quality. Also MP3 files have a weird split second delay in the beginning, which can make it tedious to line up properly in your session.

Q: Do you run any deals/discounts?

A: Generally no. But I might run temporary discounts from time to time, follow me on social media so you won't miss it. And since I don't run any bulk deals, you can contact me personally if you are looking to purchase multiple beats (3+) at once. We can then work out a better deal for you.

Q: Do you also offer mixing services?

A: Yes. Contact me on any of my social media accounts or via email for more info.

Q: Do you use samples?

A: Very rarely. And if I do, they're completely royalty-free.

Q: Where can I check out some tracks you produced?

A: I created this playlist on YouTube with a couple songs we produced. If you made a song using one of our beats and it's not in this playlist, feel free to send me the link so I can add it.

Q: Can I get a custom made beat?

A: At the moment I'm busy keeping up with producing beats for me to upload. So I can't promise you that I'll be able to make it, but if you are a comitted artist and know what kind of beat you'd like (preferably with examples), I'm sure we can work something out. Just contact me through the contact form or via social media.
