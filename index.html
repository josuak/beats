Q&A

Q: Will my beat have any voice tags (e.g. "Jozu going crazy") after purchase?

A: No. All beats will be delivered completely untagged with every lease, but if you'd like to use a version with a single tag in the beginning, just hmu and I'll gladly send it to you (tag is also included in the trackouts).

Q: Is there a limit on how many times my song can be streamed/sold?

A: No. Every license, exclusive or non-exclusive, is unlimited regarding the number of streams, sales, radio stations and paid performances.

Q: What's the difference between MP3 and WAV?

A: The MP3 files you'll get from us are high quality (320kbps), but still MP3 files are inherently compressed and therefore will have a slight quality loss. WAV files on the other hand are inherently uncompressed/lossless, meaning there's no loss in quality. Also MP3 files have a weird split second delay in the beginning, which can make it tedious to line up properly in your session. The WAV will directly line up perfectly, when setting the correct BPM.

Q: Do you run any deals/discounts?

A: Generally no. But I might run temporary discounts from time to time, follow me on social media so you won't miss it. And since I don't run any bulk deals, you can contact me personally if you are looking to purchase multiple beats (3+) at once. We can then work out a better deal for you.

Q: Do you also offer mixing services?

A: Yes. Contact me on any of my social media accounts or via email for more info.

Q: Do you use samples?

A: Very rarely. And if I do, they're completely royalty-free. If in the future, I should use a sample, I'll make it clearly recongnizable in the title.

Q: Where can I check out some tracks you produced?

A: I created this playlist on YouTube with a couple songs we produced. If you made a song using one of our beats and it's not in this playlist, feel free to send me the link so I can add it.

Q: Can I get a custom made beat?

A: At the moment I'm busy keeping up with producing beats to upload. So I can't promise you that I'll be able to make it, but if you are a comitted artist and know what kind of beat you'd like (preferably with examples), I'm sure we can work something out. Just contact me through the contact form or via social media.
